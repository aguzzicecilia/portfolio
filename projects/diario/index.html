“Il valore dei miei 20 anni” is a personal project dedicated to the redesign and reinterpretation of my great-uncle’s handwritten diary from WWI, a fragile document that captures the emotional landscape of a young man entering adulthood in the midst of conflict.

Through typography, layout, and careful archival attention, the project seeks to honor and preserve a collection of memories that are at once tender, intimate, and devastating—revealing the human dimension behind a historical event often told only through facts and dates.

The work was made possible thanks to the generosity of Wise Type for the use of WT Kormelink, and the photographic contribution of Pietro Aguzzi and Matok Lab, whose images help contextualize and enrich the visual narrative.
