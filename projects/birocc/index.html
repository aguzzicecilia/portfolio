BIROCC in the Lomellino dialect is a two-wheeled cart, usually pulled by animals such as oxen or mules, that was once used by farmers to transport hay, wood, or other goods in the fields and on farms. Today, however, it can also be used figuratively to describe an old, shabby, and rickety vehicle that can no longer properly fulfill its function.

This is the commuter bike built in 2025 from the frame of a bicycle that had been abandoned for about 20 years in a house, left exposed to the elements. Each frame is stripped, filled, and repainted by me, in addition to the careful selection of individual components and the assembly, which is partly carried out with the help of a local bike mechanic (Cicli Bandi).
