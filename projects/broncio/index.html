Broncio is an independent label founded in Vigevano (PV) in 2023 from the merger of the Emmelunga collective, active in event organization, and the Broncio project itself, which focuses on music production and artistic curation.

Broncio aims to be a key reference point for the underground music scene in the province, creating a unique space for emerging and innovative artists. The label stands out for its support of talents such as Mofw, Cecile, Moha111, and Land0000, who, through their productions and collaborations, help shape the project’s sonic and visual identity.
