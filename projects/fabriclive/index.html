Fabric Live is a series of DJ mix compilations produced by the renowned London club Fabric. Launched in 2001, the series stands out for its selections and mixes that explore electronic genres such as techno, drum & bass, grime, dubstep, and UK garage, reflecting the sounds of the club’s nights. Each volume is curated by a different DJ, including legendary names like Carl Cox, Richie Hawtin, and Skream, and it has become a point of reference for the underground music scene, particularly in the UK. Listen on Spotify

In this personal rebranding proposal, each vinyl has as its cover a general view of all the tracks included in the mix, arranged according to their duration and the value of the decibels. On the back of the vinyl you can find the songs contained in the compilation displayed on the basis of three values: bpm, energy and danceability.
